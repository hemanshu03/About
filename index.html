BIO

I am a tech enthusiast with a strong foundation in Python programming, complemented by a keen interest in C++, Computer Hardware, Networking Technologies, Cyber Security, and IoT. My technical skills are self-taught, honed through a blend of curiosity and independent problem-solving. I believe in probing and troubleshooting both software and hardware issues myself, turning to the internet and AI tools for assistance if problem proves to be challenging. Beyond technology, my creative pursuits include Nature Photography and Videography, and I have a musical side, evidenced by my piano playing skills and an ‘A’ grade for exam conducted by Gandharva Sangeet Vidyalaya, Mumbai.
I value honesty and openness, and while I am always learning, I am committed to applying my skills and passion to contribute meaningfully.
